About Me

I'm a junior at Tufts University in Medford, MA pursuing a B.S. in Electrical Engineering. I'm passionate about combining my skills in hardware design, software design, control theory and signal processing to create complex automated systems. In particular, I'm interested in product design in the robotics/automation and audio industries.

Outside of class, I serve as the Rules and Safety Officer for Tufts Electric Racing team. Before this, I was a Low Voltage Project Lead and an Electrical Technician. I also serve as Vice President of Tufts Public Harmony, a community service focused music group and play drums in an indie rock band Cedar.

Paper Airplanes

An exploration of physical design and AI-generated instructions.

Description

This project involved following AI-generated instructions to build a paper airplane. The instructions were generally clear, but there was a slight ambiguity in the first step regarding the initial fold direction ("hamburger" vs. "hotdog"). After clarifying this step, I was able to successfully create a classic paper airplane. This activity highlighted the importance of clear, unambiguous instructions, even from an AI, and the process of interpreting and refining those instructions to achieve a physical outcome.

Steps Taken:

  1. Fold the paper in half lengthwise.
  2. Create the nose of the plane by folding the top corners down to the center crease.
  3. Refine the nose by folding the angled edges down again.
  4. Fold the plane in half along the original center crease.
  5. Form the wings by folding the top edges down to align with the bottom.
  6. Adjust the wings slightly to aid with lift and stability.
